*Time Limit Notes:
  • Time limits trigger automatic notifications and escalations
  • At 80% of max time: Warning notification to case worker
  • At 100% of max time: Escalation to supervisor
  • Supervisor approval required to exceed time limits

Sub-statuses

Pending - Awaiting Consent/Initial Assessment:

  • Consent forms sent
  • Consent received, assessment pending
  • Awaiting guardian/POA response (if applicable)
  • Documentation incomplete

Declined:

  • Did not meet criteria (specify: age, location, service type)
  • Referred to another organisation (capture organisation name)
  • Capacity/resources unavailable
  • Client declined services after assessment
  • Other (requires description)

Closed:

  • Declined consent (before service start)
  • Onward referral to external organization
  • Administrative closure (duplicate referral)
  • Client unreachable after multiple attempts

Service Episode Complete:

  • By mutual consent (goals achieved)
  • Client/Whānau withdrew consent
  • Not contactable (specify attempts: 3+ over 14 days)
  • Client moved out of area
  • Referred to external provider (capture details)
  • Maximum service duration reached
  • Client deceased
  • Aged out of program
  • Other (requires description)
